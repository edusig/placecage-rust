Place Cage

Basic Usage

Just add your desired image size (width & height) after our URL, and you'll get a image of cage with that size.

https://placecage.eduardociciliato.com.br/300/200

Change the subject

Add your desired subject before the width and height.

Available subjects:

  • cage
  • murray
  • segall
https://placecage.eduardociciliato.com.br/murray/300/200

Cage variations

Add your desired variation after cage.

Available variations:

  • default
  • crazy
  • gif
https://placecage.eduardociciliato.com.br/cage/gif/300/200

Why?

After the end of the Heroku free plan the original https://www.placecage.com website went down. (See github issue about it)

I used it a lot while working with frontend before the company designer decided the final images and it was really easy to spot and replace this kind of placeholder before deploying to production.

Contribute

If you would like to have more images or support for more variations or image effects (i.e. blur, grayscale, etc...) feel free to make a contribution to the official github repo.

This is also a learning exercise for me to learn a little more Rust XD.
